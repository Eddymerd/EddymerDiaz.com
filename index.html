Hi, I'm Eddymer.

I'm a Front-End Web Developer with a background in visual arts. I'm based in northern New Jersey, love photography and I've never met a dog I didnt like. Seriously... my friends are staging a pup-intervention as we speak. My interest in web development had it's start back in the days of liveJournal and Myspace (if you're interested in hearing more of my backstory, click here). I went on to minor in Computer Science in college but, as happens to many of us, I ended up in a different field after graduation. A few years into

my career, I decided to make a shift back into tech and found an online school named Skillcrush. I brushed up on my html and CSS and learned Javascript and JQuery. I am currently learning Ruby, have many more programming languages to learn and projects in mind. (If you're interested, follow my coding adventures on the blog.) Feel free to take a look around and learn a little about me, my work, and how I can help you succeed in the digital realm and beyond.
